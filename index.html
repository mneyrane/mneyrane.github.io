About me

Hello, I am Maksym (Max), and welcome to my academic and industry portfolio website!

I am currently a PhD student at Simon Fraser University in mathematics, with Ben Adcock as my research supervisor. My research topic, which is in development and subject to change, examines adaptive sampling applied to operator learning.

I have previously completed a MSc in applied mathematics from Simon Fraser University, where I successfully defended my master's thesis on March 3, 2023. Prior to that, I worked as a data scientist for Statistics Canada for about 1.5 years in conjunction with completing an undergraduate degree in mathematics.
