About me

Hello and welcome! I am Maksym (or Max). I am currently a MSc student at Simon Fraser University in applied mathematics, progressing towards completing a master's thesis. My MSc program is supervised under professors Ben Adcock (supervisor) and Nilima Nigam (committee). The thesis topic is in deep learning for compressive imaging, though I hold general interest in machine learning, mathematical analysis and various other topics in mathematics. During my program this year, I was the recipient of the NSERC CGS-M and BC Graduate scholarship, valued at $17,500 and $15,000 (CAD), respectively.
